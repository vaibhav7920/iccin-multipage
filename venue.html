BHAGWAN PARSHURAM INSTITUTE OF TECNOLOGY
Bhagwan Parshuram Institute of Technology (BPIT) is a pioneer Institute providing education to the students of Delhi and outside Delhi in the field of engineering and management since more than a decade. BPIT is the only self financing institute affiliated to GGSIP University whose all under graduate (UG) engineering programs (B.Tech. CSE/IT/ ECE / EEE) are accredited by NBA (National Board of Accreditation) which in itself certifies the quality policy and standards followed in BPIT. All the labs in BPIT are well equipped. The fully dedicated placement team of BPIT has assured the placement of BPIT students in reputed companies such as Amazon, Zomato, Virtusa, ByJuYâ€™s, LibSys, IBM, Samsung, Motherson Sumi, Sopera Steria and many more. BPIT is spread in an area of about 5.7 acres and has four storey building of academic and office complex with lifts. A girl student's hostel having 64 double occupancy rooms, with all modern amenities has been constructed and furnished.
COMPUTER SCIENCE & ENGINEERING DEPARTMENT
The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) is the multitalented and versatile, department of college and caters to the needs of other departments as well. B.Tech CSE Programme of the department has recently been accredited by NBA (National Board of Accreditation). The department strives to prepare its students to be independent and innovative. The Department provides a propitious, environment for academic growth to its faculty and students. Regular upgrading and expansion of the labs is done as per the requirement of the industry. Exposure to latest developments and technologies is a mandatory exercise for both faculty and students. Continuous research work and live projects are taken up by the department. Numerous professional activities are conducted to improve interaction between students, faculty, other academic institutions and industries.
Getting to BPIT
Bangalore is well connected by road, rail and air.

Flying in: The nearest airport is Indira Gandhi International Airport which is one hour drive by road. The airport serves as a hub for AirAsia India, Alliance Air, Jet Airways, IndiGo, Air India and a focus city for SpiceJet.
